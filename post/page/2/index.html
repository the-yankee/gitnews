Congress - Passes Bill to Require Extended Gun Background Checks

February 28, 2019 Congress
On February 28th, Congress passed a bill, requiring all gun manufacturers to take extended background checks with all gun sales. Rep. Mike Thomas, in district CA, and Pete King, New York Representative, proposed the bill, seeking reform with illegal and unlicensed firearms. This act clamps down on drug dealers, restricting all from transferring illegal or unlicensed firearms to third parties until a background check has been completed. Nowadays, current laws allow the sale to still commence, given that the provided dealer’s discretion is approved, even if the background check is not completed in a 3 days time span.
