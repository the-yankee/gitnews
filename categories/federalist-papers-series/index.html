Federalist Papers Series - Federalist No. 46 - The American Gun Problem

March 12, 2019 Federalist Papers Series
Preamble: The Federalist Papers are a series of essays, describing the rationale and legitimacy for a new American Constitution. The writers, Alexander Hamilton, James Madison, and John Jay strived to bring upon a new era of liberty and truth in their newly founded nation. During this time, the state of their nation was nothing less of disunified nation states, living separately of each other. The new constitution was a way of uniting the states, founding the United States of America.
